This folder contains curated source packets for strategic weapons modernization, nuclear deterrence, missile defense, arms-control erosion, counterspace convergence, WMD-related concerns, and homeland missile-warning research.

These packets organize evidence for strategic analysis. They do not provide recommendations, targeting support, collection tasking, operational planning, tactical guidance, weapons employment guidance, vulnerability analysis, deployment schedules, system-performance exploitation, or technical instructions.
