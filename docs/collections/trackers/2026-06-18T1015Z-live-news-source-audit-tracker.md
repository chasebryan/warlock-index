**Source base:** WARLOCK-INDEX LIVE-NEWS page; LIVE-NEWS source-treatment
note; Research and Media Source Register; official live pages and reviewed
candidate routes for public broadcasters, institutional video services,
commercial current-event channels, and public-affairs sources.

**Analytic confidence:** Moderate for source-routing status, inclusion rules,
and current public-card/candidate classification. Low for long-term access,
advertising behavior, region availability, or source usability because live
pages can change without notice.

**Purpose:** Maintain LIVE-NEWS as a curated source system rather than an
arbitrary link list. This tracker records which live sources are public-card
approved, which are reviewed candidates, which need cleaner routes, and what
source-treatment limits apply.

**Boundary:** Source hygiene only. This tracker does not endorse providers,
rate editorial independence, bypass access controls, rebroadcast streams,
embed players, provide legal advice, or convert live observations into
durable corpus claims without follow-on corroboration.

## Bottom Line

LIVE-NEWS should keep a small public card grid and a larger docs-only audit
queue. Public-card sources need an official free route, low-friction access,
and clear source treatment. Candidate sources can be useful for monitoring or
future source work, but they should stay out of the public LIVE-NEWS grid until
their route is checked and the ad/paywall/authentication friction is acceptable.

LiveNOW from FOX now belongs in the public grid as a commercial current-event
awareness lane. Al Jazeera English, PBS NewsHour, C-SPAN, Reuters, AP, Sky
News, and BBC live routes remain candidate lanes until a later review confirms
clean official routes and appropriate public-page treatment.

## Maintenance Rules

- Recheck public-card links after major site redesigns, high-traffic crisis
  events, regional-access changes, cookie-wall changes, or video-platform
  changes.
- Keep public-card entries small and source-diverse; do not add every useful
  candidate to the public grid.
- Preserve reviewed candidates in docs so future source work is not lost.
- Move a candidate to public-card approved only after official URL, free
  access, low-friction behavior, and source-treatment label are confirmed.
- Remove or demote public-card sources that become account-required,
  paywall-first, ad-heavy, misleading, or unstable.
- For any live observation, record provider, URL, UTC timestamp, program or
  event title, observed claim, and follow-on corroborating source.
