## Bottom Line

The 2026-06-18 remarks are a review signal, not a completed force-posture
decision. WARLOCK-INDEX should now track the review as an implementation
question: what did the Department of War formally say, what review terms are
issued, what NATO or allied governments say in response, what Congress asks,
and whether later documents change U.S. force presence, basing, access,
funding, or posture language.

Until implementation evidence appears, products should use the signal as a
caveat inside NATO, Europe-Russia, allied capacity, defense industrial base,
Ukraine-support, and U.S. global force-allocation analysis. It should not be
treated as proof of troop withdrawal, redeployment, basing change, overflight
settlement, NATO consensus, or allied readiness.
