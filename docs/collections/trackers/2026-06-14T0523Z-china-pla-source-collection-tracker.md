## Collection Rules

1. Dated official sources come first; research and media sources support but
   do not replace official source-family capture.
2. PRC issuer sources must be labeled as issuer perspective and cross-read
   before analytic judgments are strengthened.
3. Taiwan, South China Sea, cyber, space, nuclear, and defense-industrial
   lanes require explicit safety boundaries before extraction.
4. Avoid live tracking. Use dated snapshots, publication metadata, and
   source-family routing.
5. Keep "China," "CCP," "PLA," "MND," "MFA," "PAP," "China Coast Guard,"
   "maritime militia," "state-owned enterprise," "private technology firm,"
   and "official media" distinct unless a source explicitly links them.
