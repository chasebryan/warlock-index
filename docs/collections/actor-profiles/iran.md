## Core Actor Notes

- Iran's security posture centers on regime survival, deterrence, regional
  influence, and resistance to U.S. and Israeli power.
- The IRGC and IRGC-Qods Force are central to power projection and internal
  security.
- Iran's strongest defense-relevant tools are distributed across missiles,
  UAVs, maritime denial, cyber activity, proxies, intelligence activity, and
  ideological networks.
- Iran is weakened and under stress, but public U.S. intelligence still
  assesses regional power-projection and internal suppression capacity.
- Iran's WMD-related file should be handled as a current uncertainty and
  compliance concern, not as a simple binary label.
