## Assimilation Rules

1. Every new actor product connects to at least one theater and one domain.
2. Every theater product identifies cross-theater effects, even when limited.
3. Every domain product identifies homeland relevance, allied relevance, and
   industrial relevance where applicable.
4. Every current-event claim receives a date and source class.
5. Every high-confidence judgment is traceable to at least one official or
   otherwise high-reliability source.
6. Every inferred relationship is marked as analytic judgment, not fact.
7. Every product preserves the no-recommendations and no-operational-guidance
   boundary.
8. Every actor profile can be superseded by a later dated product without
   deleting the prior assessment.
