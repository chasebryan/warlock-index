# Research And Media Source Register

**UNCLASSIFIED//OPEN SOURCE**

**Prepared UTC:** 2026-06-12T23:20:35Z

Research and media sources are useful for current-event awareness, conflict
timelines, and outside analytic perspectives. They should be separated from
official-source baselines and graded explicitly.

## Institute For The Study Of War

- **Source class:** B
- **Publisher:** Institute for the Study of War
- **Use:** Conflict updates, especially Ukraine, Russia, Iran, and related
  military-political analysis.
- **Reliability note:** Specialist conflict monitor with transparent products.
  Should be cross-checked for major claims.

## Reuters

- **Source class:** C
- **Publisher:** Reuters
- **Use:** Current-event reporting and official-statement tracking.
- **Reliability note:** Strong professional reporting baseline; individual
  claims still require date, attribution, and corroboration.

- **Source class:** C
- **Publisher:** The Guardian
- **Publication date:** 2026-06-04
